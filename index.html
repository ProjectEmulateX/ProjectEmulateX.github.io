Taco

  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: 5 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Ground Beef (1lbs) x1
Onion Powder (1/2 teaspoon) x1
Garlic Salt (1/2 teaspoon) x1
Celery Salt (1/2 teaspoon) x1
Ground Cumin (1/2 teaspoon) x1
Can Tomato Sauce (8 ounces) x1

Step 1: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.

Step 2:Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.

Step 3: Season beef with onion powder, garlic salt, celery salt, and cumin.

Step 4: Pour tomato sauce over the beef, stir to coat, and simmer until thickened, slightly, about 5 minutes.

Step 5: Enjoy

Beef Enchiladas

  • Time: 45 mins
  • Serves: 8 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Ground Beef (2lbs) x1
Onion (1/4 portion) x1
Shredded Chedder Cheese (1 cup) x1
Sour Cream (1/2 cup) x1
Dried Parsley (1 tablespoon) x1
Taco Seasoning (1 tablespoon) x1
Dried Oregano (1 teaspoon) x1
Ground Black Pepper (1/2 teaspoon) x1
Enchilada Sauce (2 1/2 cups) x1
Chili Powder (1 1/2 teaspoons) x1
Clove Garlic, minced x1
Salt (1/2 teaspoon) x1
Flour Tortillas x8
Black Beans, rinsed and drained (15 ounces) x1
Sliced Black Olives, drained (1 ounce) x1
Shredded Cheddar Cheese (1/4 cup) x1

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2: Cook and stir ground beef with onion in a skillet over medium heat until meat is crumbly and no longer pink, about 10 minutes.

Step 3: Drain grease. Stir 1 cup Cheddar cheese, sour cream, parsley, taco seasoning, oregano, and black pepper into the ground beef until cheese has melted.

Step 4: Mix in enchilada sauce, chili powder, garlic, and salt; bring to a simmer, reduce heat to low, and simmer until meat sauce is slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.

Step 5: Lay a tortilla onto a work surface and spoon about 1/4 cup of meat sauce down the center of the tortilla.

Step 6: Top meat sauce with 1 tablespoon black beans and a sprinkling of black olives.

Step 7: Roll the tortilla up, enclosing the filling, and lay seam-side down into a 9x13-inch baking dish.

Step 8: Repeat with remaining tortillas.

Step 9: Spoon any remaining meat sauce over the enchiladas and scatter any remaining black beans and black olives over the top.

Step 10: Sprinkle tortillas with 1/4 cup Cheddar cheese.

Step 11: Bake in the preheated oven until cheese topping is melted and enchiladas and sauce are bubbling, 20 to 22 minutes.

Step 12: Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Step 13: Enjoy.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 2 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Slices of White Bread x4
tablespoons butter, divided x3
Slices of Cheddar Cheese x2

Step 1: Preheat skillet over medium heat.

Step 2: Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.

Step 4: Place bread butter-side-down onto skillet bottom and add 1 slice of cheese.

Step 5: Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side-up on top of sandwich.

Step 6: Grill until lightly browned and flip over; continue grilling until cheese is melted.

Step 7: Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter and slice of cheese.

Step 8: Enjoy
